How to Start

Welcome to our global community!

This is a guide that will help you to start, so you can easily share the world.

1 Search

Find the best PEOPLEWINGS can help.

On the home page or search page www.peoplewings.com/search, find the ideal PEOPLEWINGS typing where you need to share your WINGS.

2 Request

Send requests to multiple PEOPLEWINGS.

Send WINGS requests with the yellow button "request", located to the right PEOPLEWINGS search results.

We recommend that you make public requests to receive invitations from other PEOPLEWINGS. To make your public applications, you have to select the flag "Let other hosts can send me invitations". It shows in the popup "New Request" that appears when you click the yellow button "request".

3 Receive confirmation

Wait confirmation of PEOPLEWINGS

Soon, you will receive confirmation messages of acceptance from PEOPLEWINGS users, who have requested them WINGS, on the message page: www.peoplewings.com/messages.

4 "Fly"

Enjoy your flight with your new WINGS!

The sharing WINGS day is very close. However, before you have to contact the host to confirm the time and place that you will find.
